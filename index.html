CobraMenu: Keep tabs on CI Joe

CobraMenu is a simple way to keep an eye on the status of your CI Joe project. Start it up, and it stays in your menu bar, ever vigilant, reporting Joe's status and whereabouts.

Dependencies

Mac OS X 10.6.0 or higher

Install

Download CobraMenu, drag the app to Applications, double-click.
